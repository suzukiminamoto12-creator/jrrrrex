Physics

Physics is the branch of science that studies matter, energy, motion, forces, and the fundamental laws of the universe.

Classical Mechanics

Classical mechanics deals with the motion of objects under the influence of forces. It includes concepts such as velocity, acceleration, Newton’s laws of motion, and gravity.

Thermodynamics

Thermodynamics studies heat, temperature, energy transfer, and the laws governing energy conversion in physical systems.

Waves and Optics

This area focuses on wave motion, sound, light, reflection, refraction, lenses, and optical instruments.

Electricity and Magnetism

Electricity and magnetism explore electric charges, circuits, magnetic fields, electromagnetic waves, and modern technology.

Modern Physics

Modern physics includes quantum mechanics and relativity, explaining atomic behavior, subatomic particles, and the structure of space and time.
