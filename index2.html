Astronomy & Space Science

Astronomy is the scientific study of celestial objects, space, and the physical universe beyond Earth.

The Solar System

Our solar system consists of the Sun, eight planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and other celestial bodies held together by gravity.

Stars and Galaxies

Stars are massive spheres of hot gas that produce energy through nuclear fusion. Galaxies are collections of billions of stars, gas, and dust bound together by gravity.

Black Holes

Black holes are regions of space where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape. They form from the collapse of massive stars.

Space Exploration

Space exploration uses spacecraft, satellites, and telescopes to study outer space, improve technology, and expand human knowledge of the universe.
